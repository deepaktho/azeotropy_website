About Campus Ambassador Program

Through our CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAM, AZeotropy allows you to demonstrate your leadership and organisational abilities. Campus Ambassadors are AZeotropy's representatives in their respective colleges, and their job is to connect with students who are interested in participating in AZeotropy's different events, competitions, and workshops. Students from all colleges with chemical, petrochemical, or petroleum engineering programmes are encouraged to register.

Why to become a Campus Ambassador?

Ambassador of the year

We will recognize and appreciate all the effort that you will put in. One will be felicitated with the prestigious Ambassador of the year award for his/her exceptional work throughout the tenure.

Free workshops

Every Campus Ambassador can attend any 2 workshops that will be organised by AZeotropy(online) absolutely free of cost. Why not learn something valuable and interesting?

Certificates

Being actively indulged in AZeotropy, throughout the year gets you a certificate of organization

Appreciation

Bridge the gap between your college participants, authorities and team AZeotropy by the exchange of queries and information. Put your best efforts to publicize AZeotropy to end up in Top 10 and receive a Certificate of Appreciation.

Free T-Shirts

Top 10 Campus Ambassadors get free T-shirts as a souvenir to cherish the memories that will be made together.

Network

Create a network among your peers that will help build your character, build confidence and gain a perspective

Publicity

Bring Participation

Bring Participation

Chem-O-Philia

Familiarize Others
